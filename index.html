What We Do

Across the country, the UUFA provides organizational and logistical support to member teams trying to improve their game and foster involvement in the East Africa Ultimate community.

Youth Development

Encouraging youth to get involved with Ultimate early on and grow a new generation of players.

International Play

Working with the Ministry of Sport to support long-distance travel for clubs to compete on an international level.

New Club Support

Supporting newly formed clubs with logistical assistance, funding, and equipment.

Uganda National Team

Organizing a national team of players from across the country to represent Uganda in international play.
